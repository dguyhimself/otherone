In the event of Force Majeure, PrimeNets reserves the right to temporarily suspend operations to protect core system liquidity.

Privacy & Data Policy

PrimeNets adheres to strict global data protection standards (GDPR compliant) to ensure the security of our Agency Partners.

1. Information We Collect

To establish your secure node, we collect fundamental cryptographic and identification data, including your registered name, encrypted contact credentials, and TRC20/EVM blockchain wallet addresses used for automated settlements.

2. End-to-End Encryption

All database transmissions, including password hashes and private ledger balances, are secured using military-grade AES-256 encryption. We do not store plain-text passwords.
